# **CEO observations**

**DBS Group Holdings 1Q 2025 financial results May 8, 2025**

### **Strong start to the year**

- **Record total income and profit before tax, ROE at 17.3%**
- **Robust business growth**
  - o Group NII up YoY, higher loan and deposit volumes more than offset lower NIM
  - o Record fees and treasury customer sales, led by strong wealth management momentum
  - o Markets trading income highest in 12 quarters
- **Asset quality resilient, NPL ratio stable and SP of 10bp**
- **GP reserves prudently strengthened in light of tariff uncertainty**
- **Total dividend of 75 cents comprising ordinary dividend of 60 cents and Capital Return dividend of 15 cents**

## **2025 outlook**

- **Business momentum resilient in April, but risk from heightened uncertainty**
- **Group net interest income slightly above 2024 levels based on three rate cuts** 
  - o Lower Group NIM, offset by balance sheet growth
  - o Markets trading to benefit from lower funding cost
  - o Funding to be deployed into non-loan assets if loan demand weakens
- **Commercial book non-interest income growth to be mid-to high-single digits**
- **Cost-income ratio to be in low-40% range**
- **SP assumed to normalise to 17-20bp; GP reserves provide buffer**
- **Net profit to be below 2024 levels, mainly due to global minimum tax of 15%**
